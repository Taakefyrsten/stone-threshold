Drag each dropper onto a patch of its material. Pick a category above, then Add to place more samples — stack several on varied stone or soil for a better match. Pixels are classified to the nearest sampled colour (hue + brightness). Double-tap a dropper to delete it.

Runs entirely in the browser — photos are never uploaded. Brightness-only split: wet soil, shadow and light-coloured crumb can be misclassified. Triage tool, not a calibrated measurement.
